About Royal Group Of Institutions

Welcome to the Official Royal Group Of Institutions mobile app under the aegis of ROYALIANS EDUCATION.

Royal Group Of Institutions, a forward-looking, secular and modern Educational Institute was established in 2011, in Choudwar Cuttack area, with the blessings of Mr. Birupakshya Kar– an excellent endeavour from the illustrious family to the cause of holistic education in our country. This Institute is considered today as one of the most prestigious Educational Institute in Odisha.

Now Also It has opened its another branch in Cuttack ,Arundoya Market,Beside Bazar India ,Badamabadi and also it has a mission to open more and more branches in other states of India.

Royal Group Of Institutions maintains the perfect quality of Education.
